New Zealand

 
   

Introduction

New Zealand; a place where it is socially acceptable to walk around barefoot, and where the sheep outnumber the
people 4:1. You may not think it's worth the hefty sum of cash it takes to travel down to our little corner of the
planet, but you'll be sorely mistaken once you gaze upon the Alps of the South Island or the pristine beaches of the
North Island.

Just a few of the things NZ has to offer...

  • Skydiving
  • Hiking
  • Bungee Jumping
  • Skiing/Snowboarding
  • Beautiful Cuisine
  • Luging
  • Scenic Drives
  • Extreme Jet Boating
  • Wine Tasting Tours
  • Friendly Wildlife
  • Caving
