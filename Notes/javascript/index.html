Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter (2 sticks - softened)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Hide

Method

Preheat oven to 375F°.
In one bowl, mix together all dry ingredients.
In a separate bowl, cream together butter and both sugars until combined.
Add in eggs and vanilla. Beat until light and fluffy.
Incorporate dry ingredients bit by bit, folding it in.
Add chocolate chips, mix well.
Roll dough into the size of roughly a quarter of your palm and place them on the baking sheet.
Bake in oven for around 10-11 minutes. For best, soft/chewy results, take out of oven when only JUST turning pale brown.
Leave cookies on baking sheet for 2 minutes and then carefully transfer to a cooling rack.
